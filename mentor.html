Signup for Mentorship

Opportunity for professional networking and development. Nepalese Association of Florida (NAF) is introducing mentorship program for our community members taping our highly capable pool of community mentors. Mentoring is proven method and research shows that mentored individual has favorable outcomes in several areas of life including educational achievement, career exploration, career growth,Business growth and job placement attainment. Our mentor can help and support with career exploration, college entrances, job placement, IT skills, entrepreneurship & business growth and manymore. To learn more about mentoring program, please write us at mentorship@floridanepalese.org or submit the form below. we will get back to you.

ABOUT US

The history of NAF (then FNA) date back to 1992, when a handful Nepalese living in Florida gathered in Gainesville and embarked upon a journey of unknown that would continue for good till eternity. We have never been geographically bound and our philanthropic activities has extended beyond Florida to Nepal, the land where all of us are emotionally rooted.
